This passion project started due to a feeling I have always had, a feeling that would bubble in conversations and make its way into my thoughts constantly. That feeling was obsession.
Being obsessed with something usually means that you spend time, energy and effort becoming more and more familiar with it. The problem was the obsession never stuck. I couldnâ€™t commit to a single obsession. I was torn from subject to subject, movies and shows, books and music. A cartoon could become my personality for weeks, despite my better judgment. Accepting this frivolous part of me gave me an opportunity, a chance to draw parallels between the things that I loved. I would measure, compare and analyze the mediums that made me feel a tangible amount of passion and I would share that passion with whoever would listen.
I found like minded individuals, people who would analyze and critique even the things they held dear, we would talk and debate about themes and execution until we had a basis to approach a rating.
Articulation is a gift, learning how to express yourself accurately and emphatically is a process. Speaking came easy, but writing was a hurdle that must be jumped every few meters.
Hopefully the articles you find here are well written, creative and insightful. If you find something you like, or possibly a new obsession, be sure to make your way back. We hope to foster a community filled with love, appreciation and understanding. Respectfully, Authors.
